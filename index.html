About

This is a study tool created by a student to help study on Senderos. You get to get quizzed directly from the curriculum using the same vocabulary in the textbook. It is strongly recommended to use a laptop, although tablets are okay too. This works 100% without internet once you load the page(yes, every single feature).

How to use

How do you use this tool? Just click that button and you'll be there(You can come back to this site by clicking the icon on the quizzer site)! However, there are some things to keep in mind. First of all, this is designed to be on laptops and may or may not work on your phone. There are also some keyboard shortcuts:

  • Alt + Vowel(a, e, i, o, u): Inputs the accented form into the textbox. The shift key can be used to make it uppercase.
  • Alt + N: Inputs an ñ into the textbox. The shift can also be used to make this uppercase.
  • Alt + Y: Inputs a ü into the textbox. While this symbol is relatively rare in Spanish, I put it there just in case. The shift key can be used to make it uppercase.
  • Alt + !: Inputs an upside down exclamation mark.
  • Alt + ?: Inputs an upside down question mark.
  • Alt + Q: Either submits the question(if the question hasn't been answered yet) or moves on to the next question(if the result came out).
  • Alt + M: Works like the "I got this correct/wrong" button.

Also, there's an autosave feature that saves your progress, even if you close the tab. If you want to practice a different set, press "reset" to delete your progress and start from the beginning. It also alerts you if you got something confused with another question.
